I. CAPTION
REPUBLIC OF THE PHILIPPINES (Represented by the Land Tenure Administration) v. MILAGROS M. VDA. DE GARCIA, No. L-22475, June 28, 1968, Dizon, J.

II. FACTS
The Republic of the Philippines, represented by the Land Tenure Administration (plaintiff-appellant), filed an appeal against Milagros M. Vda. de Garcia (defendant-appellee) [1, 2]. During the proceedings in the Supreme Court, both parties, assisted by their respective counsel, filed a joint motion requesting the withdrawal of the appeal [2]. The parties sought to have the case remanded to the Court of First Instance of Davao, manifesting their intent to be bound by the terms and conditions of a Compromise Agreement [3]. Supporting documents, including a resolution from the Garcia Estate Tenants Association and a certification from the Davao City Assessor regarding the property price, were attached to the motion [3].

III. ISSUE/S
W/N the appeal should be dismissed based on the Compromise Agreement.

IV. RULING
**YES.** The Court found the motion in order and ruled in accordance with the Compromise Agreement submitted by the parties. Consequently, the appeal is dismissed without costs, and the record of the case is remanded to the lower court for appropriate proceedings in accordance with the terms and conditions set forth in the aforesaid Compromise Agreement [4].

V. CONCURRING/DISSENTING OPINIONS
Concepcion, C.J., Reyes, J.B.L., Makalintal, Zaldivar, Sanchez, Castro, Angeles, and Fernando, JJ., concurred [4].
