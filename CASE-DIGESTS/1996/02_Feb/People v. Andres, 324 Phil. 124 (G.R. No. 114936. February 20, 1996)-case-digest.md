**II. FACTS**
Appellant Romy Andres was charged with four separate complaints for rape against Ruwerose A. Corpuz. The Regional Trial Court of Bangui, Ilocos Norte consolidated the cases and acquitted Andres of three charges but convicted him in Criminal Case No. 776-19 for the incident committed in February 1988 [1, 2]. In this specific instance, Andres pulled the victim into an old house, removed her underwear, and had carnal knowledge of her [3].
